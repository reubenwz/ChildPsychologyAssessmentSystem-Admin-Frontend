Welcome to the CANS Management Portal

The Child and Adolescent Needs and Strengths (CANS) is an assessment tool from the United States which has been adapted and validated for use in Singapore. Designed as a communimetric tool – the CANS provides a common language to communicate about clients’ needs and strengths. This allows social service professionals to work together more effectively to improve the outcomes of clients and families served. With the CANS Online application, data can now be collected to support (i) outcome management; (ii) evaluation of services and programmes; as well as (iii) research on sector needs to inform and guide policy makers in terms of resource planning and allocation. We thank you for your patience while we continue to make enhancements to the CANS Online and look forward to serving you as an integral partner in the use of CANS in the child welfare sector.
